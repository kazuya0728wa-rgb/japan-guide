PRACTICAL TIPS
💳
IC Card (Suica / ICOCA)
Get an IC Card at any major station. Works on trains, buses, and convenience stores across all 3 cities.
💴
Cash is King
Many local restaurants and small shops don't accept cards. Use 7-Eleven or Japan Post ATMs — they accept foreign cards.
👟
Wear Easy Shoes
You'll remove shoes at many traditional restaurants and shrines. Slip-ons save you a lot of time.
📱
Google Translate
Download with Japanese offline. The camera mode reads menus in real time — incredibly useful.
🏪
Convenience Stores
7-Eleven, Lawson, FamilyMart sell great food: onigiri, hot meals, desserts. Perfect for breakfast or a quick snack.

Before You Go

⚠️ Important note

Opening hours, holidays, prices, and crowds can change. Always check Google Maps or the official website before you go. teamLab Planets and popular spots require advance booking.
